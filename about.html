Our story

Farm to Table CSA began with a simple idea: that good food should be grown close to home, with care and intention. What started as a small garden and a love for organic growing quickly blossomed into a full-fledged family farm serving the Elberta community and beyond.

Over the years, we’ve stayed true to our values, planting only USDA organic, non-GMO seeds and heirloom varieties, and keeping our growing methods clean, transparent, and sustainable. Today, our CSA model allows us to connect directly with local families, share the bounty of the land, and build a stronger, healthier food system, one harvest at a time.

We run on a Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) model in which members of our community can sign up to receive weekly shares of our harvest during the fall and spring seasons. By signing up for a CSA box from Farm to Table, you are supporting local agriculture directly without the need for a middleman.
